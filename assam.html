Popular Treks in Assam

Choose from our carefully curated selection of trekking experiences in the breathtaking landscapes of Assam.

Manas National Park Trek

Explore the UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its rare and endangered wildlife including tigers, elephants, and golden langurs.

Duration: 3 Days Difficulty: Moderate

Dzukou Valley Trek

Witness the "Valley of Flowers of the East" with its rolling hills covered in seasonal flowers and pristine streams.

Duration: 4 Days Difficulty: Moderate

Tea Garden Trails

Walk through the famous tea estates of Assam, learning about tea production and enjoying breathtaking views.

Duration: 2 Days Difficulty: Easy

Why Trek With Us?

Assam Trek is a locally owned and operated adventure company specializing in eco-friendly trekking experiences in Assam and surrounding areas.

Our guides are certified professionals with extensive knowledge of the local terrain, flora, fauna, and tribal cultures. We prioritize sustainable tourism practices that benefit local communities.

Whether you're a seasoned trekker or a beginner, we have adventures tailored to your skill level and interests. From wildlife spotting to cultural immersion, we create experiences that go beyond the ordinary.
