Beas Kund Trek

Discover the glacial origin of the Beas River with stunning views of Himalayan peaks

About Beas Kund Trek

The Beas Kund Trek is one of the most beautiful short treks from Manali, taking you to the glacial origin of the Beas River. At an altitude of 3,700 meters (12,139 feet), this trek offers spectacular views of peaks like Hanuman Tibba, Shitidhar, and Friendship Peak.

Highlights: Glacial lake, panoramic mountain views, alpine meadows, rich flora and fauna, and easy accessibility from Manali.

Trek Itinerary

Day 1: Manali to Dhundi (Drive) + Trek to Bakarthach

  • Drive from Manali to Dhundi (1.5 hours)
  • Start trek through beautiful meadows
  • Gradual ascent with views of snow-capped peaks
  • Reach Bakarthach campsite (4-5 hours trek)
  • Overnight stay in tents

Day 2: Bakarthach to Beas Kund and Return

  • Early morning start for Beas Kund
  • Moderate climb to the glacial lake
  • Explore the sacred Beas Kund
  • Return to Bakarthach (5-6 hours round trip)
  • Overnight camping

Day 3: Bakarthach to Dhundi (Trek) + Drive to Manali

  • Descend to Dhundi (3-4 hours)
  • Drive back to Manali
  • Arrive in Manali by afternoon

What to Expect

The Beas Kund trek is rated as easy to moderate difficulty. The trail offers beautiful landscapes and unique experiences:

  • Lush green meadows filled with wildflowers
  • Stunning views of seven prominent Himalayan peaks
  • The sacred Beas Kund, source of the Beas River
  • Opportunity to spot Himalayan wildlife
  • Perfect introduction to high-altitude trekking

Essential Information

Best Season: May to October

Difficulty: Easy to Moderate

Max Altitude: 3,700 meters (12,139 feet)

Trek Distance: 16 km round trip

Temperature: Day: 10-20°C, Night: 0-10°C
