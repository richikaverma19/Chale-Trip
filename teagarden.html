Our Story

Founded in 1985, Tea Garden Trails has been cultivating premium quality teas using sustainable and organic farming practices. Our gardens are located in the pristine hills where the perfect combination of altitude, soil, and climate creates teas with exceptional flavor profiles.

We believe in preserving traditional tea-making techniques while innovating to meet modern tastes. Every cup of our tea tells a story of careful cultivation, ethical sourcing, and artisanal craftsmanship.
