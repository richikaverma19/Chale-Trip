Sham Valley Trek

Experience Ladakh's "Baby Trek" - perfect for beginners with stunning landscapes and cultural encounters

About Sham Valley Trek

The Sham Valley Trek, often called the "Baby Trek" of Ladakh, is the perfect introduction to high-altitude trekking. This 3-day journey through the lower reaches of Ladakh offers breathtaking landscapes, ancient monasteries, and authentic cultural experiences without the extreme altitudes of other Ladakh treks.

Highlights: Ancient monasteries, traditional Ladakhi villages, apricot orchards, stunning rock formations, and panoramic mountain views.

Trek Itinerary

Day 1: Leh to Likir (Drive) + Trek to Yangthang

  • Drive from Leh to Likir (2 hours)
  • Visit 11th-century Likir Monastery
  • Start trek through beautiful countryside
  • Pass through traditional Ladakhi villages
  • Reach Yangthang village (4-5 hours trek)
  • Overnight homestay experience

Day 2: Yangthang to Hemis Shukpachan

  • Trek through apricot orchards and barley fields
  • Cross several small streams
  • Visit Rizong Monastery (optional detour)
  • Arrive at Hemis Shukpachan village (5-6 hours)
  • Explore local culture and traditions

Day 3: Hemis Shukpachan to Temisgam (Trek) + Drive to Leh

  • Final day of trekking through scenic valleys
  • Visit Ang village and its monastery
  • Arrive at Temisgam village (4-5 hours)
  • Drive back to Leh (2 hours)
  • Evening at leisure in Leh

What to Expect

The Sham Valley trek is rated as easy, making it perfect for beginners and families:

  • Gentle trails with minimal elevation gain
  • Authentic cultural experiences in Ladakhi villages
  • Visits to ancient Buddhist monasteries
  • Stunning views of the Stok Kangri range
  • Opportunity to experience traditional Ladakhi hospitality

Essential Information

Best Season: June to September

Difficulty: Easy (perfect for beginners)

Max Altitude: 3,800 meters (12,467 feet)

Trek Distance: 30 km (total)

Temperature: Day: 15-25°C, Night: 5-10°C
