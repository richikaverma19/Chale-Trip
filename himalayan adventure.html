Our Destinations

Manali

Gateway to numerous Himalayan treks with beautiful valleys, snow-capped peaks, and adventure sports.

  • Hampta Pass
  • Beas Kund
  • Bhrigu Lake
  • Pin Parvati Pass

Shimla

Beautiful hill station with colonial charm and access to great trekking routes in the lower Himalayas.

  • Jakhu Hill
  • Shali Tibba
  • Churdhar Peak
  • Hatu Peak

Leh-Ladakh

High altitude desert with breathtaking landscapes, Buddhist culture, and challenging treks.

  • Markha Valley
  • Stok Kangri
  • Chadar Trek
  • Nubra Valley

Meghalaya

Abode of clouds with lush green landscapes, living root bridges, and unique trekking experiences.

  • Living Root Bridges
  • Nongriat Trek
  • David Scott Trail
  • Cherrapunji Trails

Assam

Gateway to Northeast India with tea gardens, wildlife, and access to Arunachal treks.

  • Dima Hasao Treks
  • Kaziranga Trails
  • Manas National Park
  • Haflong Hill Treks
