Ranthambore's Wildlife

Discover the incredible biodiversity of Ranthambore National Park

Royal Bengal Tigers

Ranthambore is home to about 80 tigers, making it one of the best places in India to spot these magnificent creatures in the wild.

Birdlife

Over 300 species of birds including crested serpent eagles, painted storks, and the magnificent Indian peafowl.

Mammals

Leopards, sloth bears, striped hyenas, sambar deer, chital, nilgai, wild boars, and Indian foxes.

Reptiles

Marsh crocodiles, Indian pythons, cobras, kraits, and various species of turtles and lizards.

Aquatic Life

The lakes and rivers are home to various fish species and attract many water birds and animals.

Flora

Dry deciduous forests with dhok trees, banyan trees, and over 300 plant species including medicinal herbs.
