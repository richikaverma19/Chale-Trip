Rupshu Valley Trek

Remote Nomadic Adventures in Ladakh's High Plateau

Tso Moriri Lake • Best Season: June-September

About the Trek

The Rupshu Valley trek takes you through one of the most remote and culturally rich regions of Ladakh, where the Changpa nomads have roamed with their herds for centuries. This high-altitude desert trek (average elevation 4,500m) features stunning landscapes of turquoise lakes, sweeping plains, and snow-capped peaks. The highlight is the breathtaking Tso Moriri lake at 4,595m, surrounded by marshes where migratory birds and kiang (Tibetan wild ass) thrive. Crossing the 5,600m Parang La pass connects you to the Spiti Valley, making this one of Ladakh's most rewarding trans-Himalayan treks.

Trek Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Leh (3,500m)

Acclimatization day in Leh. Visit Shanti Stupa and Leh Palace. Briefing about the trek in the evening.

3,500m

Day 2: Leh to Rumtse (4,100m)

Drive through Indus valley to Rumtse (4hrs). Short acclimatization walk. Visit local monastery.

3,500m → 4,100m

Day 3: Rumtse to Kyamar (4,600m)

First trekking day through scenic valleys. Cross Kyamar pass (4,800m). Camp near freshwater stream.

4,100m → 4,600m

Day 4: Kyamar to Tisaling (4,800m)

Trek through wildflower meadows. Cross Mandalchan pass (4,900m). First views of Tso Moriri in distance.

4,600m → 4,800m

Day 5: Tisaling to Nuruchan (4,700m)

Gradual descent to Nuruchan village. Meet Changpa nomads. Possible to visit their tents and see yak herds.

4,800m → 4,700m

Day 6: Nuruchan to Rachung Karu (4,500m)

Trek along ancient salt trade route. Spectacular views of Lungser Kangri peak (6,666m). Camp near Rachung monastery.

4,700m → 4,500m

Day 7: Rachung Karu to Tso Moriri (4,595m)

Arrive at stunning Tso Moriri lake. Explore Korzok village and monastery. Rest day by the lake.

4,500m → 4,595m

Day 8: Tso Moriri to Leh

Drive back to Leh via Chumathang hot springs (8-9hrs). Celebration dinner in Leh.

4,595m → 3,500m

Day 9: Departure

Buffer day in case of road closures. Transfer to airport for departure.

3,500m

Trek Highlights

Tso Moriri Lake

One of Ladakh's most beautiful high-altitude lakes, surrounded by marshes and snow peaks. Home to migratory birds and wildlife.

Changpa Nomads

Meet the resilient Changpa people who migrate with their herds of pashmina goats and yaks across this harsh landscape.

Wildlife Encounters

Spot kiang (Tibetan wild ass), blue sheep, marmots, and numerous bird species including black-necked cranes.

Parang La Pass

The challenging 5,600m pass that connects Ladakh to Spiti Valley, offering panoramic Himalayan views.

Ancient Monasteries

Visit remote monasteries like Korzok Gompa (400 years old) and Rachung Karu, centers of Tibetan Buddhist culture.

Historic Salt Route

Follow ancient trade paths used for centuries to transport salt, wool, and other goods between Tibet and Ladakh.

Trek Preparation

Physical Fitness

  • 3-4 months of regular cardio training
  • Build to 5-6 hour hikes with 10kg pack
  • Focus on endurance over speed
  • Practice walking on uneven terrain
  • Include stair climbing in regimen

Gear Essentials

  • -10°C sleeping bag (4-season)
  • Sturdy trekking boots (broken in)
  • Layered clothing system
  • High SPF sunscreen & lip balm
  • Quality sunglasses (UV400)
  • Trekking poles (highly recommended)

Health & Safety

  • Consult doctor about altitude meds
  • Stay hydrated (4-5L water daily)
  • Pack comprehensive first aid kit
  • Get travel insurance with evacuation
  • Learn basic altitude sickness signs
