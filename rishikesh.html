Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

Yoga Capital of the World - Gateway to the Garhwal Himalayas

28°C • Sunny

Overview

Nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas along the convergence of the Ganges and Chandrabhaga Rivers, Rishikesh is a spiritual haven and adventure capital rolled into one. Known as the "Yoga Capital of the World," this sacred town attracts seekers, adventurers, and travelers from across the globe. From ancient temples and ashrams to white-water rafting and bungee jumping, Rishikesh offers a unique blend of spirituality and adrenaline. The evening Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat is a mesmerizing spectacle, while the iconic Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula suspension bridges tell tales from the Ramayana.

How to Reach Rishikesh

45 mins from Dehradun

By Air

  • Nearest airport: Jolly Grant (DED) - 35km away
  • Direct flights from Delhi (1hr flight)
  • Major airlines: IndiGo, SpiceJet
  • Taxis available (₹800-1200 to Rishikesh)
  • Alternative airport: Delhi (240km away)
6-7 hrs from Delhi

By Train

  • Direct trains from Delhi (Nanda Devi Express - 6hrs)
  • Other options: Shatabdi Express, Jan Shatabdi
  • Rishikesh Railway Station (RKSH) is well-connected
  • Auto-rickshaws available from station
  • Pre-paid taxi stand at station (₹200-300 to city center)
5-6 hrs from Delhi

By Road

  • Well-connected via NH334 and NH7
  • Delhi-Rishikesh via Meerut, Muzaffarnagar, Haridwar
  • Volvo/AC buses from Delhi (₹600-1000 per person)
  • Private taxi options (₹4000-6000 one way)
  • Major bus operators: Uttarakhand Transport, RedBus

Must-Visit Attractions

Top Rated

Laxman Jhula & Ram Jhula

Iconic suspension bridges over the Ganges, steeped in mythology. Laxman Jhula (450ft long) is pedestrian-only with shops and cafes lining both sides. The area is the spiritual and commercial hub of Rishikesh.

4.8 (3,450 reviews)
Free Entry
Spiritual

Triveni Ghat

Most sacred bathing ghat where three holy rivers meet. The evening Ganga Aarti (7pm daily) is a mesmerizing spectacle with floating lamps, chanting, and devotional songs attracting hundreds daily.

4.9 (4,210 reviews)
Free Entry
Adventure

White Water Rafting

World-class rapids from Grade I to IV. The 16km stretch from Shivpuri to Rishikesh is most popular (Grade III rapids). Best season is Sep-Nov when water levels are ideal.

4.9 (3,890 reviews)
800-1500

Beatles Ashram

Abandoned ashram where The Beatles stayed in 1968. Features psychedelic murals, meditation domes, and graffiti art. The forest has reclaimed much of the area creating a surreal atmosphere.

4.5 (2,650 reviews)
150 (Entry)
Yoga

Parmarth Niketan

Largest ashram in Rishikesh with 1,000 rooms. Offers yoga/meditation courses and famous evening Ganga Aarti. The lush gardens and riverside setting create a peaceful retreat.

4.7 (3,120 reviews)
Donation Basis
Adventure

Jumpin Heights (Bungee)

India's highest bungee jumping platform (83m) located 30km from Rishikesh. Also offers giant swing and flying fox. Operated to international safety standards with professional instructors.

4.8 (2,420 reviews)
3,500 (Jump)

Popular Activities in Rishikesh

White Water Rafting

Experience thrilling rapids from Grade I to IV on the Ganges. The 16km stretch from Shivpuri to Rishikesh is most popular with 10 major rapids including Roller Coaster and Golf Course.

Shivpuri to Rishikesh

Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat

Mesmerizing evening ritual with chanting, devotional songs, and floating lamps. Arrive early for best seating. Photography allowed but maintain silence during the ceremony.

Triveni Ghat

Yoga & Meditation Classes

Choose from beginner to advanced classes at numerous ashrams. Popular options include Sivananda Ashram, Parmarth Niketan, and Rishikesh Yog Peeth. Early morning classes by the Ganges are magical.

Various Ashrams

Bungee Jumping

Plunge 83m from India's highest bungee platform at Jumpin Heights. Includes safety briefing, equipment, and certificate. Also try the giant swing (89m arc) for added adrenaline.

Jumpin Heights, Mohan Chatti

Neer Garh Waterfall Trek

Moderate 2km trek through forest to beautiful multi-tiered waterfall. Ideal for swimming in natural pools. Combine with visit to Beatles Ashram for full-day excursion.

Near Laxman Jhula

Temple Circuit

Visit sacred sites including Trimbakeshwar Temple (12th century), Bharat Mandir (oldest in Rishikesh), and Geeta Bhawan (massive complex with free accommodation and meals).

Across Rishikesh

Best Time to Visit

Autumn

20°C - 30°C
Sep - Nov
Ideal for rafting with perfect water levels. Pleasant weather for outdoor activities and spiritual pursuits.

Winter

10°C - 20°C
Dec - Jan
Cool days and chilly nights. Great for yoga retreats with fewer crowds. Ganga Aarti is magical in winter mist.

Spring

15°C - 28°C
Feb - Apr
Perfect weather before summer heat. International Yoga Festival in March attracts global participants.

Summer/Monsoon

25°C - 38°C
May - Aug
Hot with occasional rains. Rafting may be restricted during peak monsoon. Best for budget travelers.
