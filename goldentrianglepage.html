India's Golden Triangle

The Golden Triangle is India's most popular tourist circuit, connecting the capital city Delhi with Agra (home of the Taj Mahal) and Jaipur (the Pink City) in Rajasthan. This route forms a triangle on the map, covering approximately 720 km by road.

Experience the rich history, diverse cultures, and architectural wonders of North India through this unforgettable journey.

Delhi - The Capital

Delhi, India's capital territory, is a massive metropolitan area with a rich history dating back centuries. From Mughal architecture to modern skyscrapers, Delhi offers a fascinating blend of old and new.

Key Attractions:

  • Red Fort
  • Qutub Minar
  • India Gate
  • Lotus Temple
  • Humayun's Tomb

Agra - City of the Taj

Agra is home to India's most iconic monument - the Taj Mahal. This Mughal city on the banks of the Yamuna River was once the capital of the empire and boasts several UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

Key Attractions:

  • Taj Mahal
  • Agra Fort
  • Fatehpur Sikri
  • Itmad-ud-Daulah's Tomb
  • Mehtab Bagh

Jaipur - The Pink City

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is known as the Pink City for the distinctive color of its buildings. A vibrant mix of Rajput and Mughal architecture, Jaipur offers palaces, forts, and colorful bazaars.

Key Attractions:

  • Amber Fort
  • Hawa Mahal
  • City Palace
  • Jantar Mantar
  • Nahargarh Fort

Sample 6-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi

Arrive in Delhi, meet your guide. Visit Qutub Minar, Humayun's Tomb, and India Gate. Evening at leisure.

Day 2: Delhi Sightseeing

Explore Red Fort, Jama Masjid, Chandni Chowk market. Afternoon visit to Lotus Temple and Akshardham.

Day 3: Delhi to Agra

Drive to Agra (4 hours). Visit Agra Fort and Mehtab Bagh for sunset views of Taj Mahal.

Day 4: Agra to Jaipur

Sunrise visit to Taj Mahal. Drive to Jaipur via Fatehpur Sikri (6 hours).

Day 5: Jaipur Exploration

Visit Amber Fort, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, and Hawa Mahal. Explore local markets.

Day 6: Jaipur to Delhi

Morning at leisure. Drive back to Delhi (5 hours) for departure.
